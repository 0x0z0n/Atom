CropVision - AI Plant Disease Detection

Description: Uses CNNs to detect plant diseases automatically with 93%+ accuracy.

Proposed System:

  • Data Acquisition: Images collected from PlantVillage dataset.
  • Preprocessing: Images resized and normalized.
  • Feature Extraction & Classification: CNN (LeNet variant).

Architecture & Working:

  • Convolutional layers extract hierarchical features.
  • Max pooling reduces spatial dimensions.
  • Fully connected layers + Softmax activation for classification.
  • Pre-trained weights enhance accuracy.
  • Input images resized to 128×128.
  • Output: Predicted disease class with probability scores.
